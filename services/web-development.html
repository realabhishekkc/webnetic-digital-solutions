What is web development?

Web development is the craft of designing, building and maintaining the websites and web applications people use every day. It spans front-end work — the layout, design and interactions you see in the browser — and back-end work, such as databases, content management and integrations that power what happens behind the scenes.

Good web development is not just about looking the part. It is about structure, speed, accessibility and conversion: making sure the right people can find your site, navigate it effortlessly on any device, and take the action you want them to take.

At Webnetic Digital Solutions, we treat your website as a working asset rather than a digital brochure. Whether we build on WordPress or hand-code a bespoke solution, every project in Sydney is engineered to load fast, rank well and turn traffic into enquiries and sales.

Our Process

How we build your website

01

Discovery & strategy

We start by understanding your business, audience and goals. This shapes a clear sitemap, content plan and conversion strategy so every page has a purpose before a single pixel is designed.

02

Design & prototyping

We design a modern, on-brand interface and prototype the key pages. You see exactly how the site will look and feel on desktop and mobile, and we refine it together until it is right.

03

Development & build

Our developers build the site on WordPress or with custom code, writing clean, semantic markup tuned for speed, accessibility and search. Everything is responsive and mobile-first from the outset.

04

Testing & optimisation

We test across browsers and devices, optimise Core Web Vitals, check accessibility and validate forms and integrations so your site is reliable and fast the moment it goes live.

05

Launch & ongoing care

We deploy, monitor and support your site after launch with updates, security and performance care plans, turning it into a compounding growth asset rather than a one-off project.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How much does a website cost in Sydney?
It depends on scope. A focused marketing or brochure site typically starts in the low thousands, while custom WordPress builds, e-commerce and bespoke web applications cost more. Webnetic Digital Solutions scopes every Sydney project to your goals and gives you a fixed, transparent quote before any work begins.
Do you build on WordPress or custom code?
Both. We recommend WordPress when you want an easy-to-edit content site, and hand-coded or headless builds when performance and bespoke functionality matter most. Webnetic Digital Solutions chooses the stack that best fits your business rather than forcing every client into one platform.
How long does it take to build a website?
A standard business website usually takes four to eight weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on the number of pages, custom features and how quickly content and feedback are provided. We share a clear timeline up front so you always know what happens next.
Will my website be mobile-friendly and fast?
Yes. Every Webnetic Digital Solutions build is mobile-first and tuned for Core Web Vitals, with optimised images, clean code and fast hosting. The result is a site that loads quickly on phones and ranks better because Google rewards speed and good user experience.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?
Yes. We offer care plans covering updates, security, backups, performance monitoring and content changes. As a Sydney-based partner, Webnetic Digital Solutions stays involved after launch so your website keeps performing and growing over time.
